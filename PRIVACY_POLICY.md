**Last Updated: February 2, 2026**

Welcome to Little Things ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your family. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

**By using Little Things, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

- Photos of your child

**Authentication Data:**

- Session information

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or family photos to third parties.

**With Your Village Members:**

**With Service Providers:**
- **Firebase (Google):** Cloud storage, authentication, database

- These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data

**For Legal Reasons:**
- If required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or property
- To prevent fraud or abuse
- In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition)

**With Your Consent:**
- Any other sharing will only occur with your explicit permission

- All data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL)
- All data encrypted at rest (AES-256)

**Access Controls:**
- Multi-factor authentication available
- Role-based access for Village members
- Regular security audits
- Automatic session timeout

- 24/7 security monitoring
- Intrusion detection systems
- Regular vulnerability assessments

### Data Retention

**Active Accounts:**

**Inactive Accounts:**
- Accounts inactive for 2 years may be archived
- You'll receive email notification before archival
- Data can be restored upon request

**Deleted Accounts:**
- Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- Backups purged within 90 days
