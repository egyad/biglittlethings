## Getting Started

### What is Big Little Things?

Big Little Things is a mobile app designed to help parents capture, organize, and cherish daily moments with their children. From photos and voice notes to growth tracking and milestone memories, we make it easy to preserve your family's precious moments.

### How do I get started?

1. **Download the app** from the App Store or Google Play
2. **Create an account** using email, Google, or Apple Sign-In
3. **Add your child's profile** with their name and birthdate
4. **Start capturing moments** - take photos, record voice notes, and track growth
5. **Explore premium features** with a free trial

A: The app offers both free and premium tiers. The free version allows you to explore the app, while premium unlocks unlimited children, village members, photo storage, and advanced features.

## Subscription & Billing

### Subscription Plans

We offer two premium subscription options:

- **Monthly Premium**: $4.99/month
- **Annual Premium**: $49.99/year (Save 17%!)

### Managing Your Subscription

**Q: How do I subscribe to premium?**

1. Open the app
2. Tap on "Settings" or "Upgrade to Premium"
3. Choose your preferred plan (Monthly or Annual)
4. Complete the purchase through the App Store or Google Play

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**

**For iOS (iPhone/iPad):**
1. Open the Settings app on your device
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "Big Little Things"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

**For Android:**
1. Open the Google Play Store app
2. Tap Menu ‚Üí Subscriptions
3. Select "Big Little Things"
4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

**Q: Will I lose my data if I cancel?**

A: No! Your data is safe. If you cancel, you'll retain access to premium features until the end of your billing period. After that, you'll revert to the free tier, but all your memories remain accessible.

**Q: How do I restore my subscription on a new device?**

1. Open the app on your new device
2. Sign in with the same account
3. Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription
4. Tap "Restore Purchases"

Your premium subscription will be restored automatically.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**

Refunds are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) according to their policies. Generally:
- **iOS**: Refunds may be available within 14 days of purchase
- **Android**: Refunds may be available within 48 hours of purchase

To request a refund:
- **iOS**: Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
- **Android**: Visit [Google Play Help](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637)

**Q: I was charged but don't have premium access. What should I do?**

1. Try "Restore Purchases" in Settings
2. Ensure you're signed in with the correct account
3. Restart the app
4. If the issue persists, contact us with your receipt

## Technical Issues

### Common Issues

**Q: The app won't open or keeps crashing**

Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Check for app updates in the App Store/Play Store
4. Uninstall and reinstall the app (your data is safe in the cloud)

**Q: Photos aren't uploading**

1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure you have storage space on your device
3. Check app permissions (Settings ‚Üí Big Little Things ‚Üí Photos)
4. Try uploading one photo at a time
5. Restart the app

**Q: I can't sign in**

1. Verify you're using the correct sign-in method (Email, Google, or Apple)
2. Check your internet connection
3. If using email, try "Forgot Password"
4. Clear app cache and try again
5. Try signing in on a different device

**Q: Voice notes aren't recording**

1. Check microphone permissions (Settings ‚Üí Big Little Things ‚Üí Microphone)
2. Ensure your device isn't in silent mode
3. Try recording a short test note
4. Restart the app

**Q: Sync issues between devices**

1. Ensure both devices are connected to the internet
2. Sign out and sign back in on both devices
3. Force close and reopen the app
4. Wait a few minutes for sync to complete

**Q: The app is running slowly**

1. Close other apps running in the background
2. Restart your device
3. Clear app cache
4. Check for app updates
5. Ensure you have adequate storage space
